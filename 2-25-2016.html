MHacks 7: Refactor
February 25, 2016

MHacks 7: Refactor was my first exposure to the hackathon scene. Hosted by the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA, it was not only my first hackathon but also, my first time at U of M.

Having never gone to a hackathon before, I was excited but also very nervous about the whole concept. I am a big believer in sleep and in my mind, that seemed to contrast the general atmosphere of any hackathon. I made the decision to sleep no later than 4 am for both nights and I'm really I did.

Things I'd recommend bringing:

  • A pillow
  • A blanket/sleeping bag
  • A change of clothes

    • Now onto the actual event. I took a bus from the University of Toronto to the University of Michigan which took approximately 8 hours to get there. By the time we arrived, it was about 7:30 pm. First order of business was registration and dinner.
